Activating the Interactive Tools for 2D Plots When a plot frame is selected, several plotting tools become visible just below other toolbars at the top of the screen.
 Hover over a tool with your mouse for a tool tips description. To display plotting tools Click the plot. Reset Viewpoint Click the Reset Viewpoint tool to reset the viewpoint to a default position. [ Return to beginning of section ] Zooming In and Out The Zooming tool shows a small arrow pointing up and a larger arrow pointing down. To zoom in or out Click the Zooming Tool. Move the mouse pointer over the view. Click and drag towards the top of the screen to zoom out, or Click and drag towards the bottom of the screen to zoom in. As you click and drag, a zoom rectangle will appear inside the plot window. If you click and drag towards the top of the screen, when you release the mouse button the original plot will fill the zoom rectangle and additional parts of the plot will become visible to fill the plot window. If you click and drag towards the bottom of the screen, when you release the mouse button the visible plot inside the zoom rectangle will expand to fill the plot window. [ Return to beginning of section ] Translating the View Changing the Plot Intervals reveals different portions of a plot. To see different portions of a plot in an interactive way, you can translate the view with the Moving Tool . The Moving Tool shows four arrows, similar to the north, south, east, west arrows commonly shown on a map. To translate the view Select the Moving Tool. Move the mouse pointer over the view. Click and drag in the direction you want to translate the plot. As you click and drag, a moving rectangle will indicate where the translated plot will appear when the mouse button is released. Plot Coordinates The default tool for 2D plots is the Query Tool. This is used to view the coordinates of points on a plot. To view plot coordinates Select the Query Tool. Move the mouse pointer to a point of interest on the plot. Press and hold the left mouse button to view of coordinates. [ Return to beginning of section ]
