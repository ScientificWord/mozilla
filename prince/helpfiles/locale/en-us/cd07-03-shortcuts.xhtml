Automatic Substitutions Automatic substitutions are available when in text mode, math mode, or both text and math. A list of the predefined automatic substituions is included here, separated according to if they are available in text mode, both text and math modes, or math mode. The automatic substitutions can be customized or disabled in text mode or in math mode, or both using the Automatic substitution dialog. Text Automatic Substitutions
