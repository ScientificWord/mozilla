The Laplace transform depends on the function f and the number s . The equation above also defines the Laplace operator ℒ . A constant coefficient linear differential equation in f ⁡ ( t ) is transformed into an algebraic equation in f ^ ⁡ ( s ) by the operator ℒ . A solution can be found to the differential equation by first solving the algebraic equation to find f ^ ⁡ ( s ) and then applying the inverse Laplace transform to determine f ⁡ ( t ) from f ^ ⁡ ( s ) .
