Creating Animated 2D Plots

Animated Inequality Plots To plot an animated inequality Enter an expression of the form g ⁡ ( x , y , t ) < h ⁡ ( x , y , t ) . With the insert point in the expression, choose Compute > Plot 2D Animated > Inequality.
