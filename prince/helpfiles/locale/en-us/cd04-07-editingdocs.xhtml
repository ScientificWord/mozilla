Adding Non-Text Elements In addition to text, you can add images, tables, horizontal rules (lines), frames, and notes. ImagesAdding Images imageYou may insert graphic images in your documents. Version 6 supports current standard graphics file formats .pdf, .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .eps, .ps, .gif, .tif, .tiff, .svg, .bmp, .wmf, and .emf. Some formats that were supported previously have been dropped in Version 6 because they have turned out to be non-standard. To insert an image Place the insertion point where you want the graphic. Choose Insert → Image..., or click the Image icon
