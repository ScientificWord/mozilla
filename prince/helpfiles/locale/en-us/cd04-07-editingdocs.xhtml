Adding Non-Text Elements In addition to text, you can add horizontal rules (lines), tables, and images. LinesAdding Lines Although the document style determines where lines typically appear in your document, you may want to add solid lines to set off certain portions of the text. You can specify the color, width, height, and lift—the position of the line in relation to the baseline of the surrounding text. To add a line lines Choose Insert → Line... The Lines dialog box appears.
