Entering Mathematics and Text

During information entry you are either entering text or mathematics, and the results obtained from keystrokes and other user interface actions will differ depending on whether you are entering text or math. Thus we refer to being in either text mode or math mode. The default state is text mode; it is easy to toggle between the two modes and it is also easy to determine what mode you are in.
