Matrix Algebra

A rectangular array of mathematical expressions is called a matrix. The algebra of matrices provides a model for the study of vector spaces and linear transformations. A matrix with m rows and n columns is called an m × n matrix. Matrices are sometimes referred to simply as arrays , and an m × 1 or 1 × n array is also called a vector. Entries in matrices can be real or complex numbers, or mathematical expressions with real or complex coefficients. Most of the choices from the Matrices submenu operate on both real and complex matrices.
