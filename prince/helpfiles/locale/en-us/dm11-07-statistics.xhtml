Random Numbers

The random-number generators on the Statistics submenu give you a set of random numbers from one of several families of distribution functions. The choices in the dialog are Beta, Binomial, Cauchy, Chi-Square, Exponential, F, Gamma, Normal, Poisson, Student's t, Uniform, and Weibull. To generate a list of random numbers Choose Compute > Statistics > Random Numbers. Choose a distribution from the dialog, specify how many random numbers you want, and enter appropriate parameters.
