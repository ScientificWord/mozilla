Welcome to Version 6!
This Software Thinks Like You Do! Scientific WorkPlace (SWP), Scientific Word (SW), and Scientific Notebook (SNB) makes writing, sharing, and doing mathematics easier than you ever imagined. You can create attractive documents which combine text, graphics, and mathematics in natural notation.Version 6 capabilities include unlimited Undo and an updated program window.
LATEX Typesetting Version 6 of SWP and SW enables both professionals and support staff to produce beautifully typeset text and mathematics, adhering to internationally accepted standards. Stunning books and articles can be produced without the author having to know TEX or LATEX, the industry standard for mathematics typesetting. The resulting LATEX files can be submitted electronically to many journals. The program includes many document shells that have been designed to meet the typesetting requirements of specific journals and institutions. Recent advances in TEX and LATEX, including X ETEX, are included in Version 6. SNB does not produce typeset output.
Multiple Platforms Version 6 runs natively on Windows and macOS. You can share documents with colleagues regardless of operating systems. With SWP and SW you can typeset LATEX files to PDF creating fully hyperlinked documents with embedded graphics. Using SWP, SW, or SNB you can also export web pages. You can easily build an entire website with mathematical content which can be read with any web browser, such as Firefox, that displays MathML.
