Toolbar and Keyboard Shortcuts for Compute Menu Commands

Tooltips identify the buttons on the toolbars in the program window. To see the button name, hold the mouse pointer on a toolbar button for several seconds until the tooltip appears.

 Following are Math Toolbar and keyboard shortcuts for commands on the Compute menu.
