Trigonometric Identities

This section illustrates the effects of some operations on trigonometric functions. First, simplifications and expansions of various trigonometric expressions illustrate many of the familiar trigonometric identities.

There are many possible forms for trigonometric expressions. To get expressions in the form you want, you can experiment with different combinations of commands.
