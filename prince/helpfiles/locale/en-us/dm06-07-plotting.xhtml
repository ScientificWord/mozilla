Animated 3D Plots in Rectangular Coordinates To make an animated plot in rectangular coordinates Type an expression in three variables. With the insert point in the expression, choose Compute > Plot 3D Animated > Rectangular. The next example shows a 3D animated plot with Boxed Axes and Intervals − 6 ≤ x ≤ 6 , − 6 ≤ y ≤ 6 , − 3 ≤ t ≤ 3 , where t is the animation variable. The surface changes from a hyperbolic paraboloid ( t < 0 ), to a paraboloid ( t = 0 ), then to a hyperbolic paraboloid with a different orientation ( t > 0 ). Compute > Plot 3D Animated > Rectangular x 2 + y 2 + t ⁢ x ⁢ y Click this plot.
